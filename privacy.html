Privacy Policy

We don’t store creative files. Ever. dataraiils processes uploaded assets in-memory to validate, tag, and package them — then discards them immediately after use. No files are saved or shared.

We log usage data (e.g., timestamps, validation flags, file types) to improve product performance and support future enhancements. This data does not include file content.

We comply with all standard GDPR and CCPA expectations. You can contact privacy@dataraiils.ai at any time to request access or deletion of usage logs associated with your account.

This policy may evolve, but our principle won’t: your data is your business, not ours.
