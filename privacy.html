Privacy Policy

At dataraiils.ai, your data privacy is not just a priority — it’s a principle. We built this platform inside the pain of pharma ad ops, where compliance and confidentiality are essential.

No File Storage

We do not store creative files. All uploads are processed temporarily and automatically deleted after processing. Your assets are never indexed, saved, or reused.

Data Collected

We collect minimal, non-identifiable usage data to improve system performance. This includes:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Anonymized usage analytics (e.g. errors, session timing)

Email & Communication

If you contact us or join the waitlist, your email is stored securely and never sold. We may contact you regarding updates or product access. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Third-Party Tools

We use privacy-focused analytics (e.g. Plausible or PostHog) with anonymization enabled. We do not use third-party cookies or fingerprinting scripts.

Compliance

dataraiils is designed for pharma and regulated industries. We follow best practices aligned with HIPAA, GDPR, and industry-specific compliance standards.
